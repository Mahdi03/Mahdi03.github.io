   What a wonderful place to be in. Such places you'll be able to see!!

If you want to know the places to go in Bishop, you've come to the right place!

First Stop: Food!!!

Best Places to Eat:

  1. Schatt's Bakery

    2. Really famous place to go to. Great sandwiches for lunch. And don't forget to take some cookies and some Specialty Sheepherder's Bread as a souvenir!!

  2. Schatt's Roadhouse

    3. Really great burgers. Great place for dinner. Has live performances from time to time.

  3. Whiskey Creek

    4. A leisurely place to dine out.

Second Stop: Places

Best Places to Go:

  1. Ancient Bristlecone Pine Forest

    2. Did you know, the oldest tree in the world resides there!?!

  2. Law's Museum

    3. A Museum that will show you life in the olden days.

  3. Death Valley

  4. Sequoia National Forest

  5. Bodie National Park

Third Stop: Activities

  1. Hiking in the Eastern Sierra

  2. Biking on Steep Roads

  3. Mountain/Dirt Biking at Tungsten Hills

  4. Bowling

    5. There is a great Bowling Alley with food that is impressive. I hear that the Mozzarella Sticks are the best.

  5. Movies

    6. There is a Twin-Theater with a huge assortment of snacks. They show up-to-date Movies too.

  6. Freestyle Skiing/Snowboarding
