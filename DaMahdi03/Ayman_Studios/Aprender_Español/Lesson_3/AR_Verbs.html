Lesson 1

  There are three types of Spanish verbs. They are the -ar, -er, and -ir verbs. Just as in English, they need to be conjugated to be used in certain ways.

In English, you'd conjugate the verb to speak to:

  1. I speak
  2. You speak
  3. He, She speaks
  4. We speak
  5. You guys speak
  6. They speak

In Spanish, to speak is hablar. To conjugate this:

  1. Yo hablo
  2. Tu hablas
  3. El, Ella, Usted habla
  4. Nosotros hablamos
  5. Vosotros hablais
  6. Ellos, Ellas, Ustedes hablan
